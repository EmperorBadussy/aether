Seven Generative Worlds

Each visualizer is a real-time GLSL shader scene running at 60fps via Three.js. Audio-reactive to every frequency band, beat, and transient. Switch instantly or let them auto-rotate.

ELECTRIC SHEEP
Fractal flame IFS with sinusoidal/swirl variations. Bass controls iteration depth. Beats trigger variation morphs.
PARTICLE FIELD
100k+ GPU-instanced particles. Beat-reactive radial explosions.
WAVEFORM TERRAIN
3D terrain mesh displaced by live waveform data. Fly over your audio landscape.
NEURAL WEB
Force-directed graph network. Nodes pulse with assigned frequency bands. Connections flow with audio data.
VOID PULSE
Minimalist concentric rings. Maximum OLED contrast.
TRON GRID
Infinite perspective grid. BPM-synced scroll. Light columns on beats.
DNA HELIX
